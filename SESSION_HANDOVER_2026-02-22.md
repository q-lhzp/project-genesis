## 2. New Features (Phase 9 & 10)
- **Phase 10: Social Reputation & Circles:**
    - **Global Reputation:** A score from -100 (Pariah) to +100 (Icon) reflecting general public perception.
    - **Social Circles:** Standing is tracked in specific groups (Professional, Family, Friends, Underground, Public).
    - **Economic Impact:** Pariahs face surcharges in shops (+20%), while Icons receive discounts (-10%). Jobs now have minimum reputation requirements.
    - **Networking Impact:** High reputation attracts higher-quality contacts (Executives, City Officials).
    - **Propagation:** Local standing changes leak into the global score over time (daily sync).
- **Phase 9: Multi-Model Synergy (MAC):**
    - **Lightweight Roles:** Limbic and World Engine roles receive pruned context (JSON only) to run on cheaper models (Haiku/Flash).
    - **Decoupled Persona:** The Persona role no longer processes raw biological data; it reads emotional narratives generated by the Limbic system.
    - **Model Selection:** Users can configure specific AI models for each role via the "Genesis Lab" tab.
